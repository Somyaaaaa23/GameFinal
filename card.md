**💰 PAISA WAR**

**The Multiplayer Financial Literacy Card Game**

_Build ₹50 Lakh · Defend Your Corpus · Drain Your Opponents · Win Financial Freedom_

| **🃏 120 Situation Cards** | **⚡ 30 Action Cards** | **🛡️ 30 Defense Cards** | **🌈 10 Wild Cards** | **🌍 10 Life Events** |
| -------------------------- | ---------------------- | ----------------------- | -------------------- | --------------------- |

**\= 200 Cards Total · 2-6 Players · 45-90 Min · Age 15+ · Made for India 🇮🇳**

**📋 GAME AT A GLANCE**

| **🎯 Mission**         | Be the FIRST player to build a corpus of ₹50,00,000 without going bankrupt        |
| ---------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **💰 Starting Corpus** | ₹2,00,000 per player (use chips, tokens, or paper money)                          |
| ---                    | ---                                                                               |
| **🃏 Turn**            | Draw 1 Situation Card → Choose SAVE / INVEST / SPEND → Resolve → Play 1 hand card |
| ---                    | ---                                                                               |
| **💀 Bankruptcy**      | Corpus < ₹0 = Eliminated. Gift 1 card to a player before leaving.                 |
| ---                    | ---                                                                               |
| **🏆 Milestones**      | ₹5L → ₹10L → ₹20L → ₹35L → ₹50L. Each milestone = a power-up reward.              |
| ---                    | ---                                                                               |
| **🃏 Starting Hand**   | 3 Defense + 2 Action + 1 Wild per player. Draw replacement each turn.             |
| ---                    | ---                                                                               |
| **⏱️ Game Time**       | 45-90 minutes (up to 30 turns or first to ₹50L)                                   |
| ---                    | ---                                                                               |
